Make the nodes do the work on the card

Today, reading or editing a node means expand → “Details” → side panel — a two-hop detour — and the description is edited in a cramped inline box. These six directions kill the detour: the card answers what · where · how-urgent at a glance, expands in place for the rest, and any description blows up into a distraction-free focus modal (everything else blurred) for real writing. All tokens are pulled straight from Beacon — one accent #ff7a45, dark glass, the hashed category hues.
